Tiny choices, big impact. Color of font which displays price can have a big impact on men. Some people sound out the price in their brain. Omitting zeros can help sales. Tomato soup - simple font vs designer font. Friction
Real and perceived frictions. Staples created the easy button. But website is too high friction. Amazon has near zero friction.
Dubai - obscure switches. But customers will not be patient and spend time to figure out how to use your site.
User experience friction. Autocomplete tags reduce friction. Sometimes passwords are going overboard. Auto-logouts add friction.
Check every step of your conversion process
Imaginary friction and cognitive fluency. Choose domains and product names with simple names. More fluent sites had more revealing confession posts. Simple vs harder to read font - increased indecision. How long would it take to perform this exercise?
